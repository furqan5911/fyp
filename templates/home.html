Understanding Accidents

Why Do Accidents Happen?

Accidents are caused by a variety of factors including driver error, road conditions, vehicle malfunctions, and environmental influences.

Common Causes

Some common causes of accidents include speeding, distracted driving, driving under the influence, and reckless driving.

Prevention Tips

To reduce accidents, always follow traffic laws, avoid distractions, drive defensively, and ensure your vehicle is well-maintained.
